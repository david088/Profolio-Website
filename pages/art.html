ART

David's art is inspired by mangas (Japanese comics) as he later adds his spin into his pieces to create his original characters or recreating characters in his art style.

Though his pieces are majorly done in manga art-style, he also has an interest in photographing, abstract arts, and more recently, he learns digital art and applies it to one of his programming projects.
