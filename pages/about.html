ABOUT ME

Hey, it's David.

I am a Computer Science student studying at UCSD. Moving to the United States at age 15, with no previous experience in CS until college, going into my first CS class was somewhat fearsome, but I fall in love with coding right when I finish my first programming assignment. I love facing challenges and enjoy the moments when I cracked the code. During this process, it is never boring as we keep learning when facing any unknown, then obtaining the knowledge when concurring the obstacles. I enjoy every moment of it. That takes me to keep pursuing CS. Hoping to become a Software Engineer or a Full-Stack Engineer to apply what I learned to contribute to projects that solve problems and benefit people's lives.

Besides coding, my hobby has always been drawing. It allows me to express my mind in visual formats. Each line, color, and the different combinations. Together, they tell a story. Even the smallest details, could make the difference in day and night. I have always looked for improvement by focusing on details to elevate the visual experience and my professional field.
